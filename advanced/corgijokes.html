10. The Corgi with No Money

A Corgi walks into a bar and asks for a drink. “No problem. One water coming up, it’ll be $1.” replied the bartender. The corgi looks confused and says, “sorry, but I can’t afford that.” The Bartender asks, “What kind of corgi doesn’t have a single dollar?”

The corgi replies, “A PemBROKE corgi.”
